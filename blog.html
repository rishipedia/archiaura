Porter Square

Porter Square Redesign

The Porter Square Redesign explores industrial style forms and textures and plays with narratives unique to public transportation. The Project is located on Massachusetts Ave. and sits between Harvard Square and Davis Square in Cambridge Massachusetts.

Mountain Lodge

Mountain Lodge

The mountain lodge is an 18-story tower situated in the Swiss Alps near Trun, Switzerland. The design inserts guest suites and amenities into a slim and minimalist structure. A large oculus frames the view of both the nearby mountian and expansive Swiss landscape beyond.

Forest Cabin

Modern cabin in the woods

BIO-architects have designed this rustic modern house for a young family that wanted to live next to Pirogovo lake, in the suburbs of Moscow, Russia.

Würzburg Residence

The Würzburg Residence is a palace in Würzburg, Germany. Johann Lukas von Hildebrandt and Maximilian von Welsch, representatives of the Austrian/South German Baroque style, were involved in the construction, as well as Robert de Cotte and Germain Boffrand, who were followers of the French Style.

Mont Saint-Michel

A magical island topped by a gravity-defying medieval monastery, the Mont-Saint-Michel counts among France's most stunning sights. For centuries one of Europe's major pilgrimage destinations, this holy island is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site, as is its breathtaking bay.

Alhambra

The Alhambra is a palace and fortress complex located in Granada, Andalusia, Spain. It was originally constructed as a small fortress in 889 CE on the remains of Roman fortifications, and then largely ignored until its ruins were renovated and rebuilt in the mid-13th century by the Nasrid emir Mohammed ben Al-Ahmar of the Emirate of Granada, who built its current palace and walls with many beautiful, intricate details.
